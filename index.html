About Us

1st Plumbers Ltd is a family-run business that has been serving the South East London community with professional plumbing, heating, and drainage services for over 20 years. Our team of certified professionals is committed to providing quality workmanship and excellent customer service. We understand that plumbing emergencies can happen at any time, which is why we offer 24-hour emergency call-out services.

We take pride in our reputation and the trust our customers place in us. Unlike many companies, we do not pay for reviews. All of our reviews come from genuine customers who have experienced our services firsthand.

We understand the challenges faced by the elderly, which is why we offer personalized service and support. For elderly customers, we can create a video explaining the problem and potential solutions, providing reassurance and clarity every step of the way.

If you don't live near your elderly parent, you can trust us to keep you informed on what needs to be done. We'll provide updates and guidance to ensure peace of mind for both you and your loved ones.
